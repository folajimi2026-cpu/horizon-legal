Horizon AI Agency

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 11, 2026

Horizon AI Agency ("Horizon," "we," "us," or "our") provides AI-powered customer messaging tools for businesses, including automation delivered through WhatsApp, Instagram, email, and voice channels. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to businesses and their customers.

1. Who this policy covers

This policy applies to:

  • Business clients who use Horizon's platform to manage customer communications.
  • End customers who message a business client through a channel connected to Horizon, such as WhatsApp or Instagram.

2. Information we collect

From business clients: business name, contact details, working hours, product or service information, and any content submitted to configure the AI agent (such as a business description or custom instructions).

From end customers: messages sent to a connected business account (e.g. WhatsApp number or Instagram account), including message content, sender phone number or handle, and timestamps. We only receive messages from customers who initiate contact with the business first.

3. How we use information

  • To route customer messages to the correct business inbox.
  • To generate AI-assisted replies based on the business's own information (hours, pricing, services).
  • To record leads and conversation history in the business's own CRM/records.
  • To improve reliability and troubleshoot issues with message delivery.

We do not use customer message content to send unsolicited marketing, and we do not sell customer data to third parties.

4. WhatsApp and Meta Platform data

Where Horizon connects to the WhatsApp Business Platform on behalf of a client, we access only the data necessary to send and receive messages and manage message templates for that client's own WhatsApp Business Account. Clients retain ownership of their WhatsApp Business Account and phone number and may disconnect Horizon's access at any time. Our use of information received from WhatsApp Business Platform APIs adheres to Meta's Platform Terms and Developer Policies.

5. Data sharing

We share information only with:

  • The business client the customer is messaging (this is the core function of the service).
  • Infrastructure providers necessary to operate the platform (e.g. cloud hosting, messaging APIs), under standard confidentiality obligations.

We do not share customer data with unrelated third parties or advertisers.

6. Data retention and deletion

Conversation data is retained for as long as needed to provide the service to the business client, or until deletion is requested. Business clients or end customers may request deletion of their data by contacting us at the email below. We aim to complete deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Your rights

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw from further messaging at any time by informing the business you are messaging.

8. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data in transit and at rest, including encrypted connections and access controls on our infrastructure.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

10. Contact us

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at horzionai@gmail.com.
